Technology Opportunities

  • Opportunity 1: Time Saving
    • Since autonomous vehicles are self-driving, they can communicate with each other through a network, allowing vehicles to know busiest roads and avoid that traffic
    • Autonomous vehicles can analyse the best driving route in real-time, with approrpriate speeds and distances (even during "rush hour" where there is an increase in the number of vehicles on the road) in order to reduce traffic concerns
  • Opportunity 2: Benefits for the Disabled Community
    • This would increase employment opportunities for the disabled community (approximately 2 million individuals), and save $19 billion annually in healthcare expenditures that resulted from missed medical appointments
    • Autonomous vehicles would help disabled individuals with fulfilling their civic responsibilities and enjoy their civil rights to the fullest
    • New on-demand mobility for disabled people will reduce transportation obstacles and allow them to be more indepedent, as they would not have to wait for assistance from another individual.
  • Opportunity 3: Road Safety
    • A majority of motor vehicle crashes are due to human error, therefore it is estaimted that fully automated vehicles can reduce the vehilce crash rate by up to a staggering 90%
    • Other positive effects related to road safety include: reducing the number of alcohol-based accidents, distracted-driving accidents, and accidents due to driver fatigue
